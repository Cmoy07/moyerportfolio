About Me

Hi my name is Caden! I'm currently a Senior at Adrian High School, with aspirations of becoming a developer! My journey into the programming began when I was young child. I remember watching Minecraft gameplays on Youtube and thinking, "How in the world was this game created, and how can I make something like this?" I never really explored that curiosity until later, when I got into high school. That is when I decided to start taking classes that delve into my interests; allowing me to engage in what I love. Now, I whole-heartly believe that becoming a developer is my purpose in life.

I'm driven by the idea that I can create programs that can better peoples lives, either by giving them an escape for a few hours, or a space to help them be more productive.
